Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working together

How long does it take to build a website?

Most small business websites take 2-4 weeks from our initial planning session to launch. The timeline depends on the complexity of your site and how quickly we can gather content.

What makes you different from larger agencies?

As a one-person business, I provide direct, personal service without the overhead of larger agencies. You work directly with me throughout the entire process, ensuring clear communication and a website tailored specifically to your needs.

How are your services priced?

I offer transparent, budget-friendly pricing designed specifically for small businesses. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a clear proposal with all costs outlined—no surprises or hidden fees.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Absolutely! I build websites on user-friendly platforms and provide training so you can make basic updates yourself. For more complex changes, I'm always available to help through my ongoing support services.

What if I need help after my website launches?

Ongoing support is a core part of my business. I offer various support packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and aligned with your evolving business needs.

Do you work with businesses outside your local area?

While I primarily focus on serving local small businesses, I can work remotely with businesses in other areas. The personal approach and attention to your specific needs remains the same regardless of location.
