Local Bakery

"AGmedia understood exactly what my small bakery needed. The website is beautiful, easy to update, and has brought in so many new customers!"

— Sarah Johnson, Owner

The Challenge

Sarah's Bakery had a loyal local customer base but was struggling to attract new customers. Their previous website was outdated, difficult to update, and didn't showcase their delicious products effectively. As a busy small business owner, Sarah needed a solution that would attract customers without requiring constant technical management.

The Solution

  • Created a mobile-friendly website showcasing bakery products with beautiful imagery
  • Implemented an easy-to-update menu system so Sarah could change seasonal offerings
  • Added online order request functionality to streamline special orders

  • Provided personalized training so Sarah could make basic updates herself
